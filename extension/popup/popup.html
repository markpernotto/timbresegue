Before you start

To match tempo, genre, and era, this extension looks up each track's metadata using its ISRC code.

Third-party requests made on every track play:

  • Deezer — BPM, genre, and artist radio data

Deezer receives the ISRCs of tracks you listen to, which means it can infer your listening history.

Your Apple Music credentials never leave your device. All preference data is stored locally.
